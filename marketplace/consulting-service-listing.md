<h3>Your AI agents spend money. Who controls the wallet?</h3>

<p>AI agents are making purchasing decisions, calling paid APIs, and executing transactions autonomously. But most organizations have zero governance over how their agents spend. No per-agent budgets. No approval workflows. No audit trail. AgentRails fixes this with x402 payment infrastructure — and this 2-week assessment gets you from zero to production-ready.</p>

<h3>What you get</h3>

<h4>Week 1: Discovery &amp; Architecture</h4>

<ul>
<li><strong>Agent payment audit</strong> — Map every AI agent in your organization that makes or could make financial transactions. Identify uncontrolled spending, API key sprawl, and governance gaps.</li>
<li><strong>Threat modeling</strong> — Assess wallet drain risk, credential compromise scenarios, and anomalous spending patterns using our 4-layer defense framework (budget caps, approval workflows, audit trails, anomaly detection).</li>
<li><strong>Policy design</strong> — Design per-agent spending policies: daily/weekly/monthly budget caps, per-transaction limits, destination allowlists, time-based controls, and human-in-the-loop approval thresholds.</li>
<li><strong>Architecture blueprint</strong> — Design the integration of AgentRails x402 payment infrastructure into your existing stack, including SDK selection (LangChain, CrewAI, Semantic Kernel, or Microsoft Agent Framework), network configuration, and facilitator deployment.</li>
</ul>

<h4>Week 2: Implementation &amp; Validation</h4>

<ul>
<li><strong>Policy engine deployment</strong> — Configure and deploy spending policies, budget caps, and approval workflows in the AgentRails policy engine. Connect to your identity provider for role-based access (Admin, Finance Manager, Agent Operator, Auditor).</li>
<li><strong>SDK integration</strong> — Integrate x402 payment capabilities into 1-2 pilot agents using the appropriate AgentRails SDK for your framework.</li>
<li><strong>Dashboard setup</strong> — Deploy the AgentRails enterprise dashboard for fleet-wide agent spend visibility, anomaly detection alerts, and compliance audit trails.</li>
<li><strong>Validation &amp; load testing</strong> — Run pilot transactions on testnet, validate policy enforcement, verify audit trail completeness, and stress-test approval workflows.</li>
</ul>

<h3>Deliverables</h3>

<ul>
<li>Agent payment risk assessment report</li>
<li>Policy architecture document (per-agent budgets, approval thresholds, spending rules)</li>
<li>AgentRails platform configured with your policies and governance controls</li>
<li>1-2 pilot agents with x402 payment capability on testnet</li>
<li>Enterprise dashboard deployed with fleet monitoring and anomaly detection</li>
<li>Production migration runbook with mainnet activation checklist</li>
</ul>

<h3>Who this is for</h3>

<p>Organizations deploying AI agents that make financial transactions — calling paid APIs, purchasing data, executing trades, or processing payments. Ideal for engineering, finance, and security teams that need agent spending governance before going to production.</p>
