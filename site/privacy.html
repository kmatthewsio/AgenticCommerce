Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

AgentRails ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website www.agentrails.io and the AgentRails platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI agent payment infrastructure services, including our APIs, dashboard, and sandbox environment.

By accessing or using AgentRails, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an AgentRails account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Organization name (if applicable)
  • Password (stored in hashed form)

1.2 Payment and Transaction Data

As an AI agent payment infrastructure platform, we process and store:

  • USDC wallet addresses associated with your agents
  • On-chain transaction hashes
  • Payment amounts and timestamps
  • x402 payment protocol metadata (payer addresses, facilitator responses)
  • Agent spending policy configurations

1.3 Usage and Analytics Data

We collect non-personally-identifiable usage data to improve our services:

  • Page views and navigation patterns via Vercel Analytics (privacy-focused, no cookies)
  • API request logs (endpoints called, response codes, timestamps)
  • Agent execution logs (actions performed, resources accessed)
  • Error and performance metrics

1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not use cookies for tracking purposes. Our website analytics are powered by Vercel Analytics, which is privacy-focused and operates without cookies or personally identifiable tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing our services: To operate the AgentRails platform, process x402 payments, manage AI agent wallets, and enforce spending policies.
  • Account management: To authenticate your identity, manage your account settings, and communicate with you about your account.
  • Transaction processing: To facilitate, verify, and settle USDC payments on supported blockchain networks (Base, Ethereum, Arc).
  • Compliance and audit: To maintain transaction records for regulatory compliance, fraud prevention, and audit purposes.
  • Platform improvement: To analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve platform reliability and performance.
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, unauthorized access, or other security incidents.

3. Blockchain Data and Public Transactions

AgentRails operates on public blockchain networks, including Base (Coinbase), Ethereum, and Arc. It is important to understand the following:

  • Blockchain transactions are public and immutable by nature. Wallet addresses and transaction hashes recorded on-chain are permanently visible to anyone.
  • We cannot delete, modify, or redact data that has been written to a public blockchain.
  • While wallet addresses are pseudonymous, they may become associated with your identity if you share them publicly or through other services.

By using AgentRails to execute on-chain transactions, you acknowledge and accept the inherently public nature of blockchain data.

4. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party service providers to deliver our platform:

Circle

Provides Developer Controlled Wallets for agent USDC custody. Circle processes wallet creation, key management, and transaction signing. Circle Privacy Policy

Coinbase

Operates the Base blockchain network used for USDC settlement. On-chain transaction data is publicly accessible. Coinbase Privacy Policy

Stripe

Handles subscription billing and payment processing for AgentRails platform plans. Stripe Privacy Policy

Vercel

Hosts our website and provides privacy-focused analytics (no cookies, no personally identifiable tracking). Vercel Privacy Policy

Microsoft

Enterprise integrations via Copilot Studio and Microsoft 365. Data shared with Microsoft is governed by your organization's Microsoft agreement. Microsoft Privacy Statement

Each third-party provider has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their policies directly.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will remove your personal account information within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations.
  • Transaction records: Kept indefinitely for audit, compliance, and regulatory purposes. On-chain transaction data cannot be deleted as it resides on public blockchains.
  • API usage logs: Retained for 90 days for operational purposes, then aggregated and anonymized.
  • Analytics data: Vercel Analytics data is aggregated and does not contain personally identifiable information.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted to and from our platform is encrypted via TLS (HTTPS).
  • Passwords are hashed using modern cryptographic algorithms and are never stored in plaintext.
  • API keys and secrets are encrypted at rest.
  • Agent wallet private keys are managed by Circle's Developer Controlled Wallets infrastructure and are never exposed to AgentRails.
  • Access to production systems is restricted and audited.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Export

You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by emailing support@agentrails.io. We will provide your data in a portable format within 30 days of your request.

7.2 Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and personal data by emailing support@agentrails.io. Please note that we may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as transaction records for audit compliance). On-chain data cannot be deleted.

7.3 Correction

You may update your account information through the AgentRails dashboard or by contacting us at support@agentrails.io.

7.4 GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@agentrails.io.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: With the third-party services listed in Section 4, solely to operate our platform.
  • Blockchain networks: Transaction data is broadcast to public blockchain networks as a necessary part of processing payments.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Safety and security: When we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, safety, or property of AgentRails, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, in which case your data would remain subject to this Privacy Policy.

9. Cookies and Tracking

AgentRails does not use cookies for advertising or user tracking. Our website analytics are provided by Vercel Analytics, which is a privacy-focused solution that does not use cookies, does not collect personally identifiable information, and is compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and PECR without requiring a cookie consent banner.

We may use essential cookies strictly for authentication and session management in the AgentRails dashboard. These are necessary for the service to function and do not track your behavior across other websites.

10. Children's Privacy

AgentRails is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@agentrails.io and we will promptly delete such information.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you via email or through the AgentRails dashboard.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
